# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Students learning Lengua de Señas Mexicana, families supporting communication at home, educators teaching LSM, and institutions evaluating inclusive learning tools. Visitors arrive looking for confidence that the product is serious, humane, accessible, and technically credible.

## Product Purpose

Señas a Voces Academy presents an ecosystem for learning and translating LSM with AI-supported software, wearable hardware, and teacher tools. The landing page should make the value clear quickly, build trust, and invite people to begin learning or contact the team.

## Brand Personality

Warm, precise, kinetic. The brand should feel inclusive and human without becoming childish, and technological without becoming cold.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI-purple SaaS gradients, flat three-card template layouts, overused emoji-first feature cards, sterile corporate edtech pages, and motion that distracts from accessibility. Avoid changing the section order or using a visual language that competes with the existing hand/book/wave logo.

## Design Principles

Show hands, waves, learning, and connection as a single visual system.

Let motion explain flow, attention, and communication instead of decorating every element.

Keep copy specific and direct so families and institutions understand the product quickly.

Use premium craft to signal trust, but preserve warmth and legibility for a broad audience.

Respect the existing brand tokens while making the layout feel optically balanced and memorable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-visible focus states, readable line lengths, explicit form labels, and reduced-motion fallbacks for parallax, scrubbed animation, and decorative loops. Because the audience includes deaf and hard-of-hearing users and accessibility-focused institutions, clarity and inclusivity are core product requirements, not optional polish.
